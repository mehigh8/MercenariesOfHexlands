## Overview
Mercenaries of Hexlands is a turn-based multiplayer RPG which can be played in 2-4 players where each player must complete the goal first or defeat the others in combat.

**Universe and story**

The game takes place in a fantasy world filled with magic and mystical creatures. The people of this world find themselves in a medieval era, which can be seen through the architecture and tools they use.

One day, the royal family places a very important mission, and multiple mercenaries want to complete it no matter the cost, in order to be recognised by the royal family. Because of this, the mercenaries will meet one another causing them to clash.

**Flow**

Upon creating a match, each player will choose their starting equipment and will be randomly placed on the map. They can obtain better equipment by exploring the environment they are in, from cities or from enemies (either players or AI). During a turn, players can perform a number of actions based on different criteria (equipment, level, etc.) from the following options: movement, using consumables, using abilities, or interacting with a city (using services, purchasing goods, or obtaining secondary objectives). During exploration, players must complete the chosen objective to win the match. There are also additional criteria by which a player can win the match: by defeating the other players, or by having the highest number of points when time runs out.

**Mechanics**

- Winning each match is determined by completing the objective chosen at the beginning of the match, defeating all players, or accumulating the highest score when time runs out. Some examples of objectives are:
  - Defeating a Boss visible throughout the match by each player. Before he can be attacked, players will have to infiltrate his castle.
  - Finding and delivering an artifact. Once the artifact is in the possession of one of the players, it will become visible to the other players.
  - Capturing the market. It involves delivering goods to most cities, with a delivery making the city part of the player's trade chain.
- Each player has a series of attributes that help them in exploration and combat.
- Battles and exploration will take place within a world based on a procedurally generated hexagonal grid.
- The game has matchmaking available to find players to form a match. Private rooms can also be created for player selection.
- At the start of the match, each player will take turns choosing a starting kit and distributing a number of attribute points.
